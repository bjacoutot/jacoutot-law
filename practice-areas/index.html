Practice Areas

Areas of
Practice

Specialized legal counsel at the intersection of constitutional law, emerging technology, and high-stakes litigation.

i.

Election
Law

Consult Bryan

Bryan Jacoutot has represented candidates, officeholders, political parties, and government bodies in some of the most consequential election disputes in Georgia and the Southeast. His election law practice spans the full range of electoral and political legal issues.

He litigates in state and federal courts and has extensive appellate experience before the Eleventh Circuit Court of Appeals. Bryan brings both deep constitutional knowledge and practical political understanding to every matter he handles.

Redistricting

Congressional and legislative redistricting, equal-population requirements, and racial and partisan gerrymandering claims.

Ballot Access

Candidate qualification, ballot access challenges, and election administration disputes.

ii.

Cryptocurrency
& Bitcoin

Consult Bryan

Bryan is among a small number of attorneys who understands Bitcoin and digital assets at the protocol level — not just as a legal abstraction. He has studied Bitcoin since its early years, written for Bitcoin Magazine, and been cited in legal and regulatory circles for his analysis of the Howey Test as applied to digital assets.

His cryptocurrency practice serves companies, founders, funds, and individuals navigating the rapidly evolving regulatory landscape for digital assets at both the state and federal level.

Regulatory Counsel

SEC, CFTC, and FinCEN compliance; licensing; and regulatory strategy for exchanges, funds, and protocols.

Corporate Formation

Entity structuring and formation for crypto-native companies, DAOs, and token issuers.

Litigation

Enforcement defense, civil disputes, and smart contract litigation in state and federal court.

iii.

General
Litigation

Consult Bryan

Beyond his specialty practices, Bryan maintains an active general civil litigation practice. He handles a broad range of commercial disputes, constitutional claims, and statutory matters in trial and appellate courts throughout Georgia and the Southeast.

Bryan brings to every case the same rigorous analytical approach and commitment to client outcomes that characterizes his work in election law and digital assets.

Commercial Disputes

Business litigation, contract disputes, and commercial tort claims in state and federal court.

Constitutional Claims

First Amendment, due process, equal protection, and Section 1983 civil rights litigation.

Appellate Practice

Briefing and argument before the Georgia Court of Appeals, Georgia Supreme Court, and Eleventh Circuit.
